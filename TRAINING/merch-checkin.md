# Merchandise Pickup — Volunteer Guide

You hand out pre-ordered merchandise (T-shirts, Souvenir Guides) and record that
each item was picked up. The extension tells you what each attendee ordered and
does the recording for you.

> Every screen shows a blue **MERCH MODE** banner and the toolbar face is
> **Connie**. If you **don't** see the MERCH MODE banner, you're in the wrong
> mode — tell your lead before continuing.

---

## The toolbar icon — what the colors mean

| Icon | Means | You do |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 **Green** | Good — ready | Continue |
| 🟡 **Yellow** | Caution / item not yet picked up | Read the screen |
| 🔴 **Red** | Stop | Ask your lead |
| ⚫ **Black/grey** | Idle — not on a merch page yet | Open the attendee in Neon |

## The steps

### 1. Open the attendee

Find the attendee in Neon and open their **account page**, then click the
**Connie** icon. It moves you to their registration automatically.

`[SCREENSHOT: MERCH MODE banner on the popup]`

### 2. Pick the right person

You'll see **"Select the attendee picking up merch:"**. Each row lists what that
person ordered (for example **T-Shirt (Unisex L)** or **Souvenir Guide**).

Find the person in front of you and click **Review Merch →**.

`[SCREENSHOT: attendee list with ordered items and Review Merch buttons]`

### 3. Check off what you're handing over

You'll see one checkbox per ordered item.

- **Tick the box** for each item you are giving them right now.
- An item that's **already checked and greyed out** with **"Already picked up:
  …"** was collected earlier — leave it alone, don't hand out a second one.

`[SCREENSHOT: checkbox list, one item already picked up]`

### 4. Confirm

Hand over the merch, then click **Confirm Pickup**. You'll see **"Pickup
Recorded ✓"** and the tool resets for the next person.

`[SCREENSHOT: Pickup Recorded confirmation]`

---

## When to call a manager / lead

- The toolbar is **red**, or you don't see the **MERCH MODE** banner.
- The attendee says they ordered something the list doesn't show.
- An item shows **"Already picked up"** but the attendee says they never got it.
- The screen says **"Attendee merch data not ready"** or **"Could not record
  pickup"** after you retry.
- Anything that looks wrong or that this guide doesn't cover.

**When in doubt, don't hand out the item — ask.**
