Merchandise Pickup

Toolbar face = Connie.
Every screen shows the blue banner below.
No banner? Tell your lead.
MERCH MODE

What the icon color means

GreenReady — continue
YellowNot yet picked up
RedStop — ask lead
GreyNot on a merch page

Steps

  1. Open the attendee. Find them in Neon, open their account page, click the Connie icon. It moves you forward automatically.
  2. Pick the right person. The list shows what each person ordered (e.g. T-Shirt (Unisex L), Souvenir Guide). Click Review Merch → on their row.
  3. Check off what you hand over. Tick the box for each item you give them now. An item already checked + greyed with "Already picked up" was collected before — leave it, don't give a second one.
  4. Confirm. Hand over the merch, click Confirm Pickup. You'll see Pickup Recorded ✓.
[SCREENSHOT: checkbox list with one item already picked up]

Call a manager / lead if…

  • The toolbar is red, or you don't see the MERCH MODE banner.
  • They say they ordered something the list doesn't show.
  • An item shows "Already picked up" but they say they never got it.
  • You see "Attendee merch data not ready" or "Could not record pickup".
When in doubt — don't hand out the item. Ask.
