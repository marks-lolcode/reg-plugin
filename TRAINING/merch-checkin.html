📋 Overview

This station hands out pre-ordered merchandise: T-shirts and Souvenir Guides. The extension reads what each attendee ordered in Neon and records the pickup date and time automatically. You check the boxes and click Confirm — that's it.

Before you start each shift
  • Confirm the toolbar face is Connie (not Reggie).
  • Every screen has a blue MERCH MODE banner.
  • If either is wrong, ask your lead to fix it on the Options page.
No age checks or holds at this station
Merch mode skips all badge-eligibility checks. You only need to confirm the attendee ordered the items and hand them over. Age ID, holds, and emergency contact are handled at the Registration (badge) check-in stations.

🪜 Steps

1
Open the attendee in Neon
Search for the attendee by name or account number. Click their name to open their account page, then click the Connie icon in the toolbar. The extension moves you to their registration automatically.
[SCREENSHOT: MERCH MODE banner on account page]
2
Pick the right person from the list
The screen shows "Select the attendee picking up merch:". Each row lists the person's name and what they ordered. Find the person standing in front of you and click Review Merch → on their row.
Attendee says their name isn't listed?
If the person isn't in the list, ask your lead — don't hand out merch until it's confirmed in the system.

Check off the items you're handing over
You'll see a checkbox for each item the attendee ordered. Tick the box for each item you hand them right now.

⚠ Greyed-out "Already picked up" items
If an item is checked, greyed out, and shows "Already picked up: [date/time]", that item was collected at an earlier visit. Don't hand out a second one. See below for what to do if the attendee disputes this.

Hand over the merch, then confirm
Give the checked items to the attendee first, then click Confirm Pickup. The extension records the date and time automatically. You'll see "Pickup Recorded ✓" for about 1 second, then the tool resets for the next person.

🔒 "Already Picked Up" — What It Means

When an item shows "Already picked up: [date and time]", Neon has a record that someone collected it. The checkbox is locked — you can't check it again.

If the attendee says they never received it:
  • Don't hand out another item on your own authority.
  • Tell your lead — they can look up the record in Neon and decide.
  • The recording includes the exact date and time, which helps resolve disputes.
⛔ Never override a locked checkbox
Handing out a second item without manager approval is against policy. Always escalate these cases.

🚨 When to Call a Manager / Lead

Stop and get help if:

  • The toolbar icon is red, or the MERCH MODE banner is missing.
  • The attendee says they ordered something that doesn't appear in the list.
  • An item shows "Already picked up" but the attendee says they never got it.
  • The screen shows "Attendee merch data not ready" or "Could not record pickup" after you retry.
  • The person's name isn't in the attendee list at all.
When in doubt — don't hand out the item. Ask first.
