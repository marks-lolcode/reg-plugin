Field Diagnostic

Compares a field dump from a Neon AttendeeEdit page against config.js and highlights anything the extension can't resolve.

How to get the field dump
  1. Open Neon and go to any attendee's AttendeeEdit page (use test event 142 to be safe).
  2. Open DevTools (F12), click the Console tab.
  3. Paste the snippet below and press Enter. It copies the result to your clipboard (and prints it).
  4. Paste the result into the box below and click Analyze.
